## Why Consultancies Choose GridLens OE

### IEEE-Standard Load Flow & Fault Analysis
Run IEEE 4-bus through 300-bus studies with one click. Validate contractor
deliverables against the same standards used by utilities worldwide, so your
stamp carries unimpeachable authority.

### Solar-Aware Hybrid Modeling
Unlike legacy tools built for pure grid systems, GridLens OE natively models
the **dynamic interaction between PDB (or equivalent national utility) feeds
and on-site solar PV arrays**. Capture:

- Irradiance variability and cloud-transient impacts
- Inverter ride-through behavior (LVRT / HVRT)
- Reverse power flow at low-load hours
- Harmonic distortion from power electronics

These are the exact failure modes that sink real refugee-camp microgrids.

### Remote-Site Realism
Model the realities of remote deployments:

- Weak-grid infeeds with high source impedance
- Long rural feeders with voltage-drop constraints
- Voltage sag on startup of large motor loads (water pumps, medical equipment)
- Thermal derating of cables and transformers at 45 °C ambient

Your reports reflect what actually happens at the end of a 40 km line in the
desert — not textbook ideals.

### Owner's-Engineer-First Workflow
Built for the OE role, not borrowed from utility planning tools:

- Contractor-scorecard templates
- Red-line comparison between tendered vs. as-built designs
- Automated deviation reports
- Defensible audit trail, not a pile of screenshots

### Solar Contribution Allocation
Proprietary dispatch-merit-order engine quantifies exactly how much demand is
served by PV vs. PDB at each hour, enabling:

- Transparent OPEX forecasts

- ROI narratives that stand up to UNHCR, World Bank, and KfW scrutiny
